What is Opinew?

Opinew is a reviews plugin for your e-commerce website. The reviews are mainly based on pictures.

Why reviews?

They are pretty important for conversion these days. Look at that:

  • Marks & Spencer sees over 400% increase in customer reviews since launching email campaign
  • 77% of UK shoppers consult reviews before buying online
    • More than half of respondents (52%) said friendsâ€™ recommendations were influential, followed by consumer reviews (48%), advertising (24%) and advice from sales assistants (22%).
  • 90% Of Customers Say Buying Decisions Are Influenced By Online Reviews
  • 88% Of Consumers Trust Online Reviews As Much As Personal Recommendations

So how does it work?

When someone receives a product from your store, we send them a notification on their phone to review it with a coupon incentive. The whole thing is gamified - the quicker the user posts a review, the more points she gets.

On your side, you just put in a small piece of code on your product pages, similar to the way you put facebook buttons or disqus comments. We work with shopify at the moment but we can help you with your specific platform.

Both good and bad reviews actually help you bring up the trust of your customers. (Bad reviews help conversion by 67%) But of course, the website is yours and no one likes cr@p. You have full control on moderating the reviews.
