What is Opinew?


Opinew is the modern way to get reviews for your products. It's a reviews plugin for your e-commerce website which is simple to install and requires no maintenance. We manage the whole process - remind your customers, moderate the reviews and display the best ones on your product page.


And the reviews are based on pictures which users do anyway these days...

What do you get


Increase conversions

High quality reviews can increase sales by up to 20 percent

Verified reviews

Not everyone can post a review - only after we know they've bought the product. Additionally our picture-based approach from our app only tightens the process.

Automatic post-purchase email

We will take care of reminding the buyers of the great benefits coming with posting a review - discounts and helping others.

Works everywhere

Cross-browser and looks great on Shopify, BigCommerce, Volusion, Magento, Wordpress & Facebook.

Automatic moderation

Having too much on your head already? We will take care of profanity filters and suggesting which reviews will help you and your customers the most.

Rich content

Over a billion photos are posted everyday on services like Snapchat, Pinterest and Instagram. Be part of the revolution - pictures and videos over dull text.
