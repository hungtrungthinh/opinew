Consumers shopping on-line cannot touch or smell products, as would be possible in traditional retail outlets, so their purchase judgments must be based on the product information presented on the Web site. On-line sellers seek to overcome this limitation by giving consumers the opportunity to share product evaluations on-line

Our Review Plugin

We pride ourselves to be more than your average review system in the following ways:

  1. First of all, we make sure that reviews are coming from real people (not bots, hackers or other malicious users of the Internet)
  2. We automatically ask your customers to leave you a review by sending them emails. By asking them specific questions about the product, they are much more likely to leave you feedback.
  3. We also make sure that these real people are the same ones who have actually purchased your product by marking reviews as verified.
  4. We remove spam, haters and the so called trolls.
  5. We let your customers express themselves with photos, videos and other interactive content. This makes reviews more visually pleasing to your other customers.
  6. Finally we rank the high quality reviews above the low quality ones while keeping it fair at the same time.
