Opinew

What is Opinew?

Opinew is a mobile app for showing of things you have that you think are awesome or suck.

Why should I bother?

Do you own things? Clothes, a phone or a watch? You can make your items do the work for you! Snapping pictures and posting your review will collect you coupons which you can then use the very next time you shop!

Who will read my reviews?

The reviews are listed on online shops websites. Business owners are in desperate need for reviews. Accidentally, buyers are too.

One review goes on many websites with the same product page. You will be helping not only the shop owner but also thousands of buyers of this product who are not sure yet whether to buy yet or search for something else.

So how does it work?

Do you read reviews before buying something online? Yes? Turns out that most of us do.
But also we rarely leave reviews.
We just can't be bothered, we forget or don't know exactly what to write.

With Opinew it's easy.

  1. Find something you already own.
  2. Snap a photo. Maybe leave a comment.
  3. Done.
You are rewarded each time you post a review of something you own.
If you buy something new, the sooner you post a review, the more rewards you can expect!
